Structural Design for 5-Story Office Building

This project involved the complete structural design and analysis for a new 5-story commercial office building. The primary challenge was to create a cost-effective and robust design that allowed for large, open-plan office spaces on each floor while adhering to strict seismic safety codes.

The final design utilized a composite steel frame with concrete slabs on metal decking. This approach provided the necessary strength and stiffness while minimizing column footprints, maximizing usable floor space. Special attention was paid to the lateral force-resisting system, which employed a series of moment-resisting frames to ensure performance during a seismic event.


Technical Details:

  • Foundation: Reinforced concrete mat foundation.
  • Superstructure: Composite steel frame with moment-resisting frames.
  • Floor System: Concrete slab on metal decking.
  • Analysis Software: ETABS, SAFE.

Throughout the construction phase, I provided engineering support, including reviewing shop drawings for structural steel and responding to contractor RFIs to ensure the design was executed as planned.
