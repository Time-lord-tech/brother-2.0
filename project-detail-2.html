Foundation and Steel Frame Design for Industrial Warehouse

This project involved the structural design for a large-scale industrial warehouse. The client required a durable structure with a large, open internal space to accommodate heavy machinery and storage racks.

The design featured a robust steel frame to allow for wide, unobstructed bays. The foundation was a key consideration due to heavy floor loads, and a specially reinforced concrete slab-on-grade was designed to meet these demands.


Technical Details:

  • Foundation: Reinforced concrete slab-on-grade.
  • Superstructure: Pre-engineered steel building frame.
  • Roof System: Standing seam metal roof.
  • Analysis Software: RISA-3D, STAAD.Pro.
