Timber Roof Design and Analysis for Community Center

This project featured the design of an exposed heavy timber roof structure for a new community center. The architectural vision called for a warm, natural aesthetic, making heavy timber the ideal material.

The design utilized glued-laminated (glulam) timber beams and columns to create a dramatic, vaulted ceiling. Careful attention was paid to the connection details to ensure they were both structurally sound and visually appealing. The result is a beautiful and functional space that highlights the natural strength of wood.


Technical Details:

  • Material: Glued-laminated (glulam) timber.
  • Connections: Custom steel knife plates.
  • Design Code: NDS for Wood Construction.
  • Analysis Software: RISA-3D, WoodWorks.
