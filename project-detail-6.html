Vibration Analysis for Heavy Machinery in Processing Plant

This project involved analyzing the structural impact of installing new, heavy vibrating machinery in an existing processing plant. The goal was to ensure the structural integrity of the floor system and surrounding frame.

A dynamic analysis was performed using Finite Element Modeling (FEM) to simulate the operational vibrations. The results indicated that the existing structure was susceptible to resonance. A tuned mass damper system was designed and installed to counteract the vibrations, ensuring the long-term safety and serviceability of the structure.


Technical Details:

  • Analysis Type: Dynamic analysis, Finite Element Modeling (FEM).
  • Solution: Tuned mass damper system.
  • Equipment: Industrial shakers and screens.
  • Analysis Software: ANSYS, SAP2000.
