Design of Long-Span Roof Trusses for Retail Center

The primary challenge for this new retail center was to create a large, column-free interior space for the main shopping hall. This required the design of efficient long-span roof trusses.

Using structural analysis software, I designed a series of lightweight steel trusses that spanned the full width of the building. The design was optimized to reduce steel tonnage and simplify fabrication and erection, resulting in significant cost savings for the client.


Technical Details:

  • Roof System: Long-span steel trusses.
  • Connections: Bolted connections for ease of assembly.
  • Design Code: AISC 360.
  • Analysis Software: RISA-3D.
